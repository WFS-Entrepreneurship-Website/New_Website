What Do We Do?

Woodberry's entrepreneurial ecosystem and alumni connections are rich and diverse, and the community around Woodberry allows clubs such as our own to connect students to graduates and form even deeper ties. We invite you to join our community and learn more about wwhat our club has to offer through this website.

Through the toils of early life many lose sight of the future ahead. Our goal is to educate students and members on entrepreneurial and business opportunities. We focus on teaching members how to manage a business, start a business from the ground up, how to work with a team, and ultimately how to be successful through teaching with hands on experience.
